Application Wireframe: The Kitchen

Interactive

The Kitchen is based on the service of the same name in Erin Morgenstern's The Starless Sea.

In the novel, visitors who stay in rooms by the Starless Sea write their requests on slips of paper and send them to The Kitchen via dumb waiter. The Kitchen replies with whatever they interpret the visitor requiring: information, food, drink, or fresh clothing.

The concept behind the application is to provide a similar sort of all-inclusive service for people who live in apartment towers.

People are obtaining more goods and services on-line, a trend which is only accelerating. But the price of doing everything by phone app or web site is that now users need to keep track of several different apps/sites for a bewildering variety of home-specific or consumer-specific goods and services.

The Kitchen is designed to be offered by a property management company or condo board. It comes pre-organized with services and information specific for each building or neighbourhood. Residents who use the app will be directed to vetted service providers who work in the area and (preferably) already know the building.
